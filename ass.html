KNOW ABOUT WEB3BRIDGE

Web3bridge is a program that trains web3 developers in africa. They train developers through remote and onsite web3 development training and they also have a proper interaction with their students through their friendly community on telegram.

Click on the link now to interact on telegram~~ click me

In addition to the trainings given, they made it easier for newbies to know about web development by bringing about the web2 training.

THE WEB2 TRAINING

The web2 training prepares us for the web3 training ahead. In the web2 training, the following courses are being taught:
  • Javascript
  • Typescript
  • HTML
  • CSS
  • React
  • GIT
  • Netlify
  • Nodejs

THE WEB3 TRAINING

After passing through the trials in the web2 training, web3 training is the next phase of life. It is the upgraded version of the web2 training. Without the web2 training, a person can't start the web3 training.
In the web3 training, the following courses are being taught:

  • Blockchain
  • Ethereum
  • Foundry
  • Solidity
  • Security
  • Ethersjs
  • Hardhat
  • Web3js
